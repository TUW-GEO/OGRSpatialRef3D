Coordinate Reference Systems (CRS)

Provide the original and target coordinate reference system (CRS) as EPSG PCS or GCS codes. Some examples are:
• 4326: Lat/Long WGS84
• 4269: Lat/Long NAD83
• 4267: Lat/Long NAD27
• 32611: UTM 11 WGS84
• 26911: UTM 11 NAD83
• 26711: UTM 11 NAD27
